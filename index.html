Step into a calm and welcoming space where you can enjoy delicious drinks while spending time with our friendly cats. Whiskers Cat Café is the perfect place to relax, unwind, and connect with animals in a warm and cosy environment.

Our café is designed to offer a peaceful escape from busy everyday life, whether you are visiting with friends, family, or simply taking time for yourself.

At Whiskers Cat Café, we place a strong focus on comfort, relaxation, and animal welfare. creating an enjoyable and memorable experience for everyone.

Welcome to Whiskers Cat Café

Why Visit Us

🛋️

A Calm Space

Soft seating, warm lighting, and a quiet atmosphere make our café the ideal spot to slow down, breathe, and let the stress of the day melt away.

We've designed every detail, from the colour of the walls to the softness of the cushions, to help you feel instantly at ease the moment you sit down.

Whether you're taking a break between meetings or simply want somewhere peaceful to spend an hour, our space is built for unhurried moments.

🐱

Friendly Cats

Every cat in our café has their own personality and story. Spend time getting to know them, and you might just find a new favourite.

From lap cats who'll happily nap beside you to playful ones who love a bit of attention, there's a companion here for every kind of visitor.

Our team is always on hand to introduce you to each cat and share a little about their background, so you feel like you're meeting old friends.

☕

Great Coffee

Enjoy freshly brewed coffee, a range of teas, and daily home-baked treats, all made to be savoured slowly alongside good company.

Our menu changes regularly to keep things interesting, with seasonal specials and a rotating selection of cakes baked fresh each morning.

Pull up a seat, order a drink, and let a purring companion join you while you enjoy a moment to yourself.
