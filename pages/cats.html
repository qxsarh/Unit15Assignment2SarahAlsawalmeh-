Meet Our Cats

Every cat at Whiskers Cat Café has their own personality, story, and favourite spot to nap. Come say hello and find out who you click with.

🐱

Luna

Friendly & Calm

Luna is a gentle grey tabby who loves curling up on laps and watching the world go by from the windowsill. She's the first to greet new visitors and the last to leave a cosy blanket. If you're after a relaxed, purring companion for your coffee break, Luna is your cat.

  • Age: 4 years
  • Favourite spot: The windowsill
  • Loves: Slow blinks and chin scratches
🐈

Milo

Playful & Energetic

Milo is a ginger bundle of energy who never met a toy he didn't like. He's usually found chasing feathers, pouncing on shadows, or trying to steal the spotlight from every other cat in the room. Perfect if you want an interactive, entertaining visit.

  • Age: 2 years
  • Favourite spot: The climbing tower
  • Loves: Feather toys and chasing laser dots
