Meet Our Cats

Every cat at Whiskers Cat Café has their own personality, story, and favourite spot to nap. Come say hello and find out who you click with.

How We Choose Our Cats

Every cat at Whiskers Cat Café comes from a local rescue shelter. We work closely with our partner shelters to welcome cats who are calm, sociable, and comfortable around people, ensuring a safe and happy environment for both our cats and our visitors.

Before joining the café, each cat has a full health check, is fully vaccinated, and is neutered. We also give them time to settle in gradually, so they're relaxed and truly ready to meet you.

Our team spends time observing each cat's personality before they join the café floor, making sure they genuinely enjoy company and handling rather than simply tolerating it. Cats who prefer a quieter life are matched with loving foster or adoptive homes instead, so every cat you meet here is truly happy to be part of the café.

We also monitor each cat's wellbeing on an ongoing basis, with regular vet check-ups and rest days away from visitors when needed. Their comfort always comes before anything else.

Many of our cats are also available for adoption. If you fall in love with one during your visit, just ask a member of staff and we'll happily talk you through the process.
