Café Rules

Our cats' comfort and safety comes first. Please follow these simple rules so everyone — cats included — has a lovely visit.

🤲

Be gentle with the cats

Let them approach you first, and always use slow, calm movements.

🚫

Do not feed them

Our cats have specific diets — please don't share your food or drink.

🧼

Wash hands before entering

Hand sanitiser is available at the entrance for everyone's safety.

📵

No flash photography

Flashes can startle the cats — regular photos are always welcome.

😴

Let sleeping cats sleep

If a cat is napping, please let them rest and find another friend to meet.
